CareCompanion

CareCompanion is a secure, patient-facing health portal that allows patients to log in and access their medical information.

Features

  • View medical records and health history
  • Review current medications and dosage information
  • Check upcoming and past appointments
  • Track vital signs and lab results

Privacy & Security

Patient data is accessed only with explicit patient authorization through secure OAuth2 authentication. Data is used solely to display health information to the authenticated patient. No patient data is shared with third parties.
