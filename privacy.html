1. Overview

CareCompanion ("we", "our", "the Service") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your health information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

2. Information We Access

When you connect your healthcare provider account, the Service may access the following information through FHIR APIs with your explicit authorization:

  • Patient demographics (name, date of birth, contact information)
  • Medical conditions and diagnoses
  • Current and past medications
  • Allergies
  • Vital signs and lab results
  • Immunization records
  • Appointments
  • Care plans and goals

3. How We Use Your Information

Your health information is used solely to:

  • Display your medical records to you within the Service
  • Provide personalized health tracking and reminders

We do not:

  • Sell your health information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your health data for marketing purposes
  • Store your healthcare portal login credentials

4. Authentication and Authorization

CareCompanion uses OAuth2 (SMART on FHIR) for authentication. You authorize access through your healthcare provider's patient portal. We receive a secure access token but never your username or password.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Encryption of data at rest
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular security assessments

6. Data Retention

Health data retrieved from your provider is used for display purposes during your active session. You may revoke access at any time through your healthcare provider's patient portal settings.

7. HIPAA Compliance

CareCompanion handles protected health information (PHI) in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and applicable state regulations.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the Service's access to your health data at any time
  • Request information about what data has been accessed
  • Request deletion of any stored data

9. Third-Party Services

The Service connects to your healthcare provider's FHIR API to retrieve your health data. Your interaction with your healthcare provider's authentication system is governed by their own privacy policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@carecompanion.app.
